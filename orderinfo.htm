Shipping Information

WE CAN NOT SHIP TO CA, AZ, HI, AK  -  NO INTERNATIONAL ORDERS PLEASE 

In an effort to limit the amount of time your plants need to stay in a box, plants are shipped only on Mondays with the exception of UPS Next Day Air, which will be shipped Mondays and Wednesdays. Order deadline for next Monday shipping is Saturday 8am.  Orders placed after that will be shipped the following Monday. 

Please be aware of your local USDA hardiness zone and your last frost free date. Many of our customers have greenhouses or other methods to over winter their plants so we trust in their knowledge as to when to ship. We do not recommend ordering plants to be shipped during a time when temperatures in your area are below freezing. Should extenuating weather conditions arise during a requested shipping period, we reserve the right to delay the shipment until the weather is more favorable. After all, we want your plants to arrive healthy and happy.

All orders will be confirmed upon shipment via e-mail.

How to Order Online

You may order securely online with Visa, Master Card, American Express or Discover Card or with a Visa or Master Card check card.

To place an order visit our shopping pages and click on the item that you would like to order. To place that item in the shopping cart, simply click on "Add Item to Shopping Cart". This will place one item in your shopping cart. You can at any time to change the quantity, delete items or check out.
